# Product Context: JTWMS

## Problem
Manual scheduling with spreadsheets causes double‑booking, under‑utilisation of FT teachers (wasted fixed salary), over‑reliance on expensive PT teachers, and reactive recruitment. There is no forward‑looking view of teacher capacity.

## Solution
A web app that:
- Maintains a single source of truth for teachers, classes, rooms.
- Automatically generates conflict‑free timetables that **fill FT teachers first**.
- Provides a 12‑week rolling forecast of teacher supply vs. demand.
- Alerts HR when centres have too many idle FT staff or too little overall capacity.
- Keeps a human approval gate on all automatically generated schedules.

## Success Metrics (KPIs from proposal, condensed)
- Timetable fill rate ≥ 95%
- Average FT utilisation rate (actual/contracted) ≥ 85%
- Underutilisation rate (<60% utilisation) below 10%
- Forecast accuracy ±10%
- Recruitment lead time < 30 days
